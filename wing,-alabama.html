Wing, Alabama

Wing is an unincorporated community in Covington County, Alabama, United States.





== Location ==

Wing is located entirely within Conecuh National Forest on Alabama State Route 137, 20.8 miles (33.5 km) south-southwest of Andalusia. Wing has a post office with ZIP code 36483.
