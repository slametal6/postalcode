Olney Springs, Colorado

Olney Springs is a statutory town in Crowley County, Colorado, United States. The population was 315 at the 2020 census.





== History ==

A post office called Olney Springs has been in operation since 1909. The town was named after one Mr. Olney, a railroad official.





== Geography ==



Olney Springs is located in southwestern Crowley County at 38°9′57″N 103°56′45″W (38.165844, -103.945723). Colorado State Highway 96 leads east 11 miles (18 km) to Ordway, the county seat, and west 38 miles (61 km) to Pueblo.

According to the United States Census Bureau, the town has a total area of 0.2 square miles (0.52 km2), all of it land.

Slightly west of the town is the Crowley County Correctional Facility, owned by the Corrections Corporation of America. It has 1,794 prisoners from various states.
