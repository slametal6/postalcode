DTDC

DTDC Express Limited, trading as DTDC (short for Desk to Desk Courier & Cargo) is an Indian courier delivery services company, headquartered in Mumbai.





== History ==

In 2012, DTDC acquired a 52 percent stake in Eurostar Express of Eurostar Group in the UAE. In April 2013, DTDC acquired 70% of Nikkos Logistics.

In June 2013, French courier company Geopost (owned by La Poste) acquired a 39% stake in DTDC from Reliance Capital's private equity arm, taking its overall ownership in DTDC to 42%.





== Sponsorships ==

In January 2003, DTDC became the title sponsor of then NFL club Tollygunge Agragami.
